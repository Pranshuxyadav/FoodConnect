Why FoodConnect?

The world faces a dual challenge of food waste and hunger. Millions of people go hungry daily while vast amounts of food are wasted. FoodConnect addresses this issue by providing a platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to NGOs and volunteers who can distribute it to those in need.

What We Offer

  • Real-Time Food Availability: Real-time updates on available food items.
  • Secure Communication: Messaging system for seamless coordination.
  • Compliance and Safety: Adheres to all food safety standards.

How It Works

Step 1: Register

Sign up as a restaurant, NGO, or volunteer.

Step 2: List and Find Food

Restaurants list surplus food, NGOs and volunteers find available food.

Step 3: Coordinate and Deliver

Use our tools to coordinate pickups and ensure timely delivery.
